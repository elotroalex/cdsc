Supporting Caribbean Digital Scholarship

Based on our experiences working with different digital projects in North America and the Caribbean, we have designed and implemented various training and outreach programs that, in turn, address local needs. These strategies work in dialogue with each other, fostering a community of trained Caribbean practitioners with a standard set of skills, ethical principles, and long-term goals.
